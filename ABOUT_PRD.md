# About Us Page — PRD

## Objective
Communicate brand identity, credibility, and enterprise capability through a clean, minimal, and professional layout.

---

## S1 — Hero Section

**Layout**
- Full viewport (100vh)
- Background image: `../../../assets/images/about-us.jpg`
- Dark overlay/gradient for readability

**Content**
- Tagline (2–3 words) — positioned top ~60%, left ~10%
- Supporting sentence below tagline

**Notes**
- High contrast text
- No heavy animations

---

## S2 — Core Content (bg: `--color-primary`)

### Sub-section 1 — Intro (Text Left, Icons Right)
- Two-column layout
- Left:
  - Eyebrow
  - Headline (2–3 lines)
  - Accent underline
  - Description (3–4 lines)
- Right:
  - 3 icon rows (icon + title + short text)

---

### Sub-section 2 — Stats Strip
- 4 equal columns (full width)
- Each:
  - Large stat number
  - Small uppercase label
- Vertical dividers between columns
- Slight bg contrast

---

### Sub-section 3 — Philosophy (Icons Left, Text Right)
- Same as Sub-section 1 but reversed

---

## S3 — Team / Experts (bg: `--color-secondary`)

**Layout**
- Grid: 2 rows × 4 columns (8 cards)
- Responsive: 4 → 2 → 1 columns

**Card**
- Image (75–80% height) — `../../../assets/images/gaurav.jpg`
- Name (heading)
- Role (small text)
- Short description (1–2 lines)

**Style**
- Card bg: `--color-primary`
- Minimal, clean, no heavy effects

---

## Responsive
- 2-column → 1-column (mobile)
- Stats: 4 → 2 columns
- Maintain spacing consistency

---

## Design Principles
- Minimal, clean, enterprise feel
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Consistent spacing
- Very limited use of accent color
- Avoid heavy animations or effects
